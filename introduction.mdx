## Overview

WiseSpark is an AI-powered chat platform that streamlines communication and project management for teams. You can leverage advanced AI models for intelligent conversations, collaborate in shared workspaces, and manage credits efficiently. Designed for productivity, WiseSpark helps you automate routine tasks and focus on high-value work.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with WiseSpark in minutes. Follow these steps to create your account and send your first message.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.wisespark.com` and create an account with your email. Verify your inbox to activate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Credits" icon="credit-card">
    Purchase credits via the billing dashboard. Start with the basic plan for testing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure AI Model" icon="settings">
    Go to Chat settings, select your preferred model (e.g., GPT-4), and adjust parameters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Chatting" icon="message-circle">
    Open a new chat, type your query, and interact with the AI. Use workspaces for team projects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  After setup, explore model settings for optimal performance. Check `/quickstart` for detailed installation guides.
</Callout>
