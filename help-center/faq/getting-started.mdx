## Quick Guide

1. Log in at `https://app.wisespark.com`.
2. Click "New Project" from your dashboard.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Click "Create" to finish setup.
5. Start adding documentation pages by clicking "Add Page".

## Tip

<Callout kind="tip">
You can invite collaborators from your project settings anytime.
</Callout>
