---
title: Cannot Save Document
description: Resolve document saving errors in WiseSpark.
---

## Symptoms

- Clicking "Save" does nothing
- Error: "Unable to save document"

## Causes

- Internet connection lost
- Session expired

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    ````bash
    ping app.wisespark.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh login">
    Log out, then log back in to WiseSpark.
  </Step>
</Steps>
