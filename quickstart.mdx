## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
You need:
- A valid email address for account verification
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An internet connection for accessing `https://app.example.com`
</Callout>

## Create Your WiseSpark Account

Follow these steps to sign up, complete the setup wizard, start chatting, and manage your initial credits.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to `https://app.example.com/signup`.

    Fill in your details:
    
    | Field       | Description                  |
    |-------------|------------------------------|
    | Email       | Your primary login email     |
    | Password    | Choose a strong password     |
    | Full Name   | Your display name            |

    Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from WiseSpark.

    Click the **Verify Email** button in the email.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Didn't receive it? Check spam/junk folders or resend from the login page.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete First-Time Login" icon="login">
    Return to `https://app.example.com/login` and sign in with your verified email and password.

    Follow the setup wizard:
    1. Select your preferred language
    2. Choose initial AI model (e.g., GPT-4o or Claude)
    3. Set workspace name (default: "My Workspace")
    4. Review terms and click **Get Started**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Your First Chat" icon="message-circle">
    From the dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com`, click **New Chat**.

    Type a prompt like: "Explain machine learning in simple terms."

    Press Enter or click the send button.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Sample Prompts" icon="sparkles">
        - "Summarize this article: [paste URL]"
        - "Generate a project plan for launching a SaaS app"
        - "Debug this JavaScript error: `Uncaught TypeError`"
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Model Selection" icon="cpu">
        Click the model dropdown in the chat interface to switch between available models.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage Credits and Quotas" icon="credit-card" default-open="true">
    View your balance in the top-right account menu.

    Initial credits: `100 free credits` (approx. 50 chats).

    | Usage     | Credits per Chat |
    |-----------|------------------|
    | Short     | 1-2              |
    | Medium    | 3-5              |
    | Long/Complex | 10+          |

    <Expandable title="Add More Credits">
      Go to **Billing** > **Add Credits**.
      Minimum: `$10` for `500 credits`.
    </Expandable>
  </Step>
</Steps>
